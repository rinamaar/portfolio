ABOUT ME

After graduating from a high school that specialises in computer science, mathematics and physics, I set out to find what floats my boat. I explored my interests in legal studies, learned German while living in Vienna and moved to London to learn how to tattoo. It was only after becoming a tattoo artist that I rediscovered and cultivated my curiosity for all things tech.

The process of designing and bringing something into existence has always been a source of fascination for me, which inspired me to embark on a year of full-time studying and intense learning while attending and completing a full-stack software engineering bootcamp.

When I'm not coding...

I annoy my cat, Yum Yum. I also enjoy running, learning about science and space, attending art exhibitions and fun events (as opposed to enjoying attending boring events?), watching tv shows and spending quality time with my friends and family.
